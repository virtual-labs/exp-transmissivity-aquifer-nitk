##### These procedure steps will be followed on the simulator

1. When you click on the Transmissivity of an Aquifer file, a new window will open as shown below.<br>
<img src="images/t1.png"><br>

2. Click on the scale to measure the internal diameter of the cylindrical mould, and then click on the NEXT button to proceed further.<br>
<img src="images/t2.png"><br>
<img src="images/t3.png"><br>

3. Click on the trowel to fill the mould with the soil sample, and then click on the NEXT button to proceed further.<br>
<img src="images/t3.png"><br>

4. Attach the mould with the two filter discs, and click on the NEXT button to proceed further.<br>
<img src="images/t4.png"><br>

5. Click on the tap to pour water into the mould, and then click on the NEXT button to proceed further.<br>
<img src="images/t5.png"><br>

6. Click on the NEXT button at the bottom-right corner to proceed to the next step.<br>
<img src="images/t6.png"><br>

7. The result will be displayed as shown below. Repeat the same procedure for Trial 2 and Trial 3.<br>
<img src="images/t7.png"><br>
